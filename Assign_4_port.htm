Background¶

This data contains electrode readings from a single primary visual cortex neuron of a mouse. The experiment presented drifting sine waves to mice with the contrast of stimuli ranging from 4% (virtually no contrast) to 100% (grating varying between black and white). These stimuli were presented under two experimental conditions.

In the control (CTRL) condition, each trial began with a 2000ms blank screen, followed by the oscillating sine wave for 1000ms at the trial's contrast level, which was then followed by 1000ms blank screen. In the adapt (ADAPT) condition, each trial began with the adaptation stimulus (a 50% contrast sine wave grating for 2000 ms), followed by the oscillating sine wave for 1000ms at the trial's contrast level, which was then followed by 1000ms blank screen.

Creating a list of condition labels¶

With such a large amount of data, using heat maps is an effective way to highlight the spike trains across all experimental conditions. We first need to use nested for loops to loop through conditions and contrast levels to create a list of each experimental condition.

In [5]:
# Conditions
cond_labels = ['CTRL', 'ADAPT'] 

# Labels for the levels of stimulus contrast
contr_labels = [4, 8, 12, 16, 24, 32, 48, 64, 84, 100]

condition_labels = pd.DataFrame([[condition, contrast] for condition in cond_labels for contrast in contr_labels ],
                               columns=['condition', 'contrast'])

Creating a new DataFrame¶

Next, we will create a DataFrame with each row consisting of a peri-stimulus time histogram. We do this by looping through experimental conditions and selecting the times where a spike (action potential) occurred.

In [6]:
psth_df_list = []
for condition in cond_labels:
    for contrast in contr_labels:
        spike_times = df['time'][(df['condition']==condition) & (df['contrast']==contrast) & (df['spike']==1)]
        hist, bins = np.histogram(spike_times,
                          bins=time_bins,
                         )
        psth_df_list.append(hist)

psth_df = pd.concat([condition_labels, pd.DataFrame(psth_df_list)], axis=1)
